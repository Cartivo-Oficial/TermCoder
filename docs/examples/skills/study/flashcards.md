---
name: flashcards
description: Turn notes or a topic into a set of study flashcards
---
Make flashcards to help the student memorize the material.

1. Work from what the student gave you (pasted notes, a file, or a named topic). If it's a
   topic, cover its most important, testable points.
2. Produce 10–20 cards as a clean **Q → A** list. Keep each question specific and each
   answer short (one fact or idea). Mix recall (definitions, dates) with understanding
   (why/how) questions.
3. Group cards under short headings if the material has clear sections.
4. Offer to save them to a file, or to quiz the student on them one at a time.
