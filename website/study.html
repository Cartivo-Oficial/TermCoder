termexplorer

A study companion in the same tool.

TermCoder ships a sister persona for schoolwork. Pick the termexplorer model and the coding agent becomes a patient tutor — in your language, no programming required.

What it does

From a blank page to ready to study.

  • Explains step by step — breaks a concept down until it clicks, at your pace.
  • Summarizes — turns notes, an article, or a chapter into clear key points.
  • Homework, worked out — shows the reasoning so you learn it, not just the answer.
  • Flashcards — question-and-answer cards from your material.
  • Practice quizzes — questions to test yourself, with feedback.
  • Study plans — a schedule that fits the time you have before the test.

How to switch

One model change.

Open the model picker and choose termexplorer/auto. From then on, just ask.

❯ /model
# choose "termexplorer/auto" under termcoder AI

❯ summarize these notes into key points for my biology test
❯ make 10 flashcards from chapter 3
❯ build me a 5-day study plan for my history exam
Who it's for

Students and friends — not just coders.

No terminal experience? That's fine. Install once, pick the study model, and type your question like you would to a tutor. It replies in the language you write in.

Install the CLI, or get the desktop app with a simplified study layout.
