The AI coding agent for your terminal.

TermCoder reads your code, runs commands, and ships changes from a fast terminal interface. It works free with no API key and no account — or bring your own model. A study mode is built in.

Why TermCoder

A terminal agent with real tools, and nothing in your way.

An agent loop that reads, edits, and runs — wrapped in a keyboard-driven interface that stays out of the way.

Free, no API key

Start immediately on a free, keyless model. No sign-up, no card, no quota to set up — open a folder and type. If it hiccups, it retries and falls back on its own.

Any model, or fully local

Bring Anthropic, OpenAI, or Gemini with your own key — or run a local Ollama model with nothing leaving your machine. Switch mid-session with /model.

Build and Plan modes

Plan mode reads and proposes without touching files; Build mode carries the change out and asks before it writes or runs. Toggle with shift+tab.

Works unattended

Hand it a goal and it plans, edits, runs your tests, and keeps fixing until they pass — checkpointing each round so you can revert.

Sub-agents and skills

Delegate focused work to reviewer, tester, debugger, and architect sub-agents. Reusable skill playbooks load on demand, only when needed.

Sync, share, and packs

Sync your setup across machines, share a session as a link, and install agents, skills, and commands as packs — all through GitHub, no server.

Classrooms

A teacher opens a class, students join with a link, and assignments and submissions ride GitHub. Async by design, nothing to host.

A study mode inside

Switch to TermExplorer and the same tool tutors schoolwork — explanations, summaries, and spaced-repetition flashcards. No coding required.

TermExplorer

A study companion in the same tool.

Pick the TermExplorer model and TermCoder explains step by step, summarizes your notes, and turns them into spaced-repetition flashcards you review with /review — plus practice quizzes and study plans, in your language.
